2020 Salon

Discussing New Ideas In Software Creation

We're a group getting together with a common belief: that the state of the art in creating software deserves a drastic overhaul, that the future belongs not to a fine-tuning of current ideas but a larger shift to something new, and we believe that there's vast potential that is still going untapped.

There's no format or agenda. We meet in Berkeley, California, the first Saturday of every month over coffee/tea to talk.

The group is not being publicized much but very open to new members. If you would like to attend, please just RSVP so that we can make sure we reserve enough space.
